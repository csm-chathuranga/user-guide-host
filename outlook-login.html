Outlook First-Time Login &
Microsoft Authenticator Setup Guide

Follow the steps below to access your Outlook email for the first time and configure Microsoft Authenticator for secure sign-in.

Steps Overview

  1. 1Open a Private Browser Window
  2. 2Go to the Outlook Sign-in Page
  3. 3Verify the Email Address
  4. 4Enter the Temporary Password
  5. 5Start Security Setup
  6. 6Install Microsoft Authenticator
  7. 7Prepare the Authenticator App
  8. 8Scan the QR Code
  9. 9Setup Complete
  10. 10Change Your Password
  11. 11Stay Signed In
  12. 12Access Outlook
1

Open a Private Browser Window

Open Google Chrome and select New Incognito Window. This ensures no existing browser session interferes with the login.

Keyboard shortcut: Press Ctrl + Shift + N to open an Incognito window instantly.
Chrome menu → New Incognito window (Ctrl+Shift+N)
2

Go to the Outlook Sign-in Page

Browse to outlook.office.com and enter the email address provided by your administrator. Click Next.

Microsoft sign-in page — enter your work email address
3

Verify the Email Address

Confirm the email address shown is correct, then click Next.

Verify your email address is correct before proceeding
4

Enter the Temporary Password

Enter the temporary password supplied by your administrator and click Sign in.

Note: You will be required to change this password after completing setup (Step 10).
Enter the temporary password provided by your administrator
5

Start Security Setup

Microsoft will display a "Let's keep your account secure" prompt. Click Next to begin the multi-factor authentication setup.

Click Next to begin setting up account security
6

Install Microsoft Authenticator

Install the Microsoft Authenticator app on your mobile device using the links shown. If already installed, select Set up a different authentication app if required, then click Next.

Download: Available on Google Play Store (Android) and Apple App Store (iOS).
Download and install Microsoft Authenticator on your mobile device
7

Prepare the Authenticator App

Open Microsoft Authenticator on your phone:

  • Allow notifications if prompted
  • Tap Add account
  • Select Work or school account

Then return to the browser and click Next.

Set up your account in the Authenticator app, then click Next
8

Scan the QR Code

Use the Microsoft Authenticator app on your phone to scan the QR code displayed on screen. After the account is added successfully, click Next.

Can't scan? Click the "Can't scan the QR code?" link on screen to enter a code manually instead.
Point your phone camera at the QR code to link the Authenticator app
9

Setup Complete

✔

Authenticator Configured Successfully

Microsoft Authenticator is now set as your default sign-in method.

A confirmation message will appear. Click Done to continue.

Setup complete — Microsoft Authenticator is now configured
10

Change Your Password

You will be required to update your password. Fill in the three fields:

  • Current password — the temporary password you received
  • New password — your chosen new password
  • Confirm password — re-enter your new password

Click Sign in when done.

Create a new secure password to replace the temporary one
11

Stay Signed In

Microsoft will ask if you want to stay signed in.

✓ Yes
Select if this is your personal work computer to reduce future sign-in prompts.
✗ No
Select if you are on a shared or public computer to keep your account secure.
Choose Yes for personal devices, No for shared/public computers
12

Access Outlook

Your Outlook mailbox will open. You are now ready to send and receive emails. Setup is complete!

Outlook inbox — you are now logged in and ready to use your email
