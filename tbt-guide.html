TBT — SMS Support Ticket System

Learn how to create and manage support tickets using simple SMS text messages.

1 Create Ticket

Text to Create Ticket

Simply send any text message to our support number. Your message becomes a support ticket automatically. You don't need to sign up or fill out any forms — just text us and we'll take care of the rest.

2 Confirmation

A support agent gets assigned

Once your ticket is created, it's automatically assigned to an agent. You'll receive a confirmation SMS with your unique Ticket ID to track your request.

3 Updated

Stay updated via SMS

Whenever your agent adds a comment or your ticket status changes, you'll get an SMS automatically. No app needed — everything comes to your phone.

4 Reply

Reply to Add a Comment

Need to add more info? Reply to the same number using this format.

Format
@ticketID your message here
Use the @ticketID format to reply with more info. Your agent is notified instantly.
Quick Reference
Create a ticket: Text anything to +1 (626) 263-4999
Add a comment: @ticketID your message
Get updates: Automatic SMS on every comment or status change
Opt out: Reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages
